The Gada Special Economic Zone (GSEZ) actively participated in the Trade and Investment Symposium held from January 22 to 24, 2025, in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, at the Skylight Hotel, aimed at a Japanese-based business delegation. Mr. Motuma Temesgen, Director General of GSEZ, delivered a comprehensive presentation outlining the zone’s key advantages, investment opportunities, and areas for potential collaboration with Japanese businesses. Additionally, as a panelist, Mr. Temesgen addressed questions from participants, further emphasizing GSEZ’s strategic role in fostering economic partnerships. The delegation was led by H.E. Daba Debele, Ethiopian Ambassador to Japan, and the event concluded with fruitful Business-to-Business (B2B) discussions, where participants exchanged valuable insights and perspectives on future investment opportunities.
