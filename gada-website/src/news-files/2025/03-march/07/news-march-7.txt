H.E. Mr. Suleyman Eshetu, Deputy Head of the Urban and Infrastructure Bureau and his delegates from various industries and institutions of the Amhara National Regional State, visited Gada Special Economic Zone (GSEZ). The GSEZ officials warmly welcomed the delegates on-site and guided them through the zone’s infrastructure developments. The visit continued at the GSEZ Head Office, where Mr. Dame Chali provided an insightful briefing on the zone’s vision and progress. Concluding the visit on a gracious note, Mr. Motuma Temesgen, CEO of GSEZ, delivered heartfelt closing remarks, expressing appreciation for the delegation’s engagement and interest.
