Gada Special Economic Zone Development Corporation (GSEZDC) made a sort of consultative meeting with Yeshi Investment Group today. Engineer Demelash Mulu, the Deputy CEO of GSEZDC welcomed and presented why zone is and what it is for in detail so that this investment group could come and invest in the zone. Mr. Robel Seido from Yeshi Investment Group presented the project this group wants to anchor in the GSEZDC. Mr. Paulo Defayeri, the Yeshi Investment Group CEO, also made a remark that he got insights and said, “we will do our best with GSEZDC in areas of investment their group will work.” In addition, Mr. Paulo said, “We will bring investors from Italy so that they could invest in GSEZDC using this opportunity.”
