Gada Special Economic Zone Development Corporation (GSEZDC) made a sort of consultative meeting with EMHE Trading PLC, the Diaspora from USA, today. Engineer Demelash Mulu, the Deputy CEO of GSEZDC welcomed and presented why zone is and what it is for in detail so that this Diaspora could come and invest in the zone. This investors were led by Mrs. Mawerdi Ahmed and properly revealed their investment and told that they will invest in the zone in Craft Paper industry soon. Mr. Ebsa Ahmed also presented their concept paper of the Craft Paper Industry and questions and answers were made. Finally, engineer Demelash Mulu, the Deputy CEO of GSEZDC, made final remarks.
