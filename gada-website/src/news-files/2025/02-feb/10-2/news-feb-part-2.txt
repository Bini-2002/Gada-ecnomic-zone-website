His Excellency Mr. Awolu Abdi, Deputy President of the Oromia National Regional State, conducted an official visit to the ongoing projects within the Gada Special Economic Zone (GSEZ) today. During his visit, he commended the progress made thus far and underscored the imperative of intensifying investor attraction efforts to accelerate the zone’s development. He further engaged with GSEZ officials, inquiring about key areas where his intervention could facilitate and enhance the zone’s growth. In response, Mr. Motuma Temesgen, CEO of GSEZ, provided a comprehensive briefing on the current status of projects and the number of investors registered to contribute to the zone’s advancement. The visit was deemed highly significant, as it reinforced the commitment of GSEZ staff to diligently drive forward the development agenda, ensuring that the zone emerges as a strategic economic hub.
