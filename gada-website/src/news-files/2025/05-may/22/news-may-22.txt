Today, we had the pleasure of hosting and engaging in a productive discussion with representatives from EUROBS COMMODITY GROUP LIMITED, a company investing in solar power plant development within the Gada Special Economic Zone (GSEZ), Ethiopia a zone uniquely dedicated to supporting the data mining industry. The discussion was held at the GSEZ headquarter, focused on aligning mutual interests and exploring collaborative opportunities in sustainable energy and technological infrastructure. Following the discussion, the delegation toured the zone and expressed strong interest in moving forward with investment plans, promising to return soon to initiate concrete steps.
