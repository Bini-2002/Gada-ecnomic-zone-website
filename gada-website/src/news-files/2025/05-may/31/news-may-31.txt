Gada Special Economic Zone (GSEZ) actively participated in the Oromia Investment Forum 2025, organized by the Oromia Investment and Industry Bureau under the compelling theme, “Unleashing Oromia’s Investment Potential: Building Trust, Improving Services, and Driving Growth,” held at the Skylight Hotel. On this notable occasion, GSEZ signed a landmark investment agreement worth USD 100 million with Hujian Investment Group for the establishment of a solar panel manufacturing facility.
