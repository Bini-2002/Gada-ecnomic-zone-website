A delegation from Huaye Mining & Processing PLC, led by H.E. Ambassador Dewano Kedir, held discussions with the Gada Special Economic Zone Development Corporation (GSEZDC) regarding imminent investment plans. Ambassador Dewano highlighted the zone’s strategic advantages and its potential to become a major investment hub. Mr. Motuma Temesgen, CEO of GSEZDC, presented the zone’s current achievements and affirmed the Corporation’s readiness to collaborate with the company.
