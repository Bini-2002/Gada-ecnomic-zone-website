Gada Special Economic Zone (GSEZ) held a consultative meeting with investors.The Gada Special Economic Zone (GSEZ) organized a consultative meeting with investors who had been operating in the area prior to the establishment of the GSEZ. The meeting took place at the Headquarters in Adama, with an opening speech delivered by Mr. Motuma Temesgen, Director General of the GSEZ. During the session, Mr. Dame Chali presented an overview of the GSEZ, including its background and the rationale behind its formation. Mr. Biruk Shawul followed with a presentation on Ethiopia’s SEZ proclamation. Investors raised various long-term concerns, which were addressed by Deputy Director General, Engineer Demelash Mulu, and Director General, Mr. Motuma Temesgen.
