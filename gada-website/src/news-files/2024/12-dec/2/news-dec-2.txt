On December 2, 2024, the Gada Special Economic Zone Development Corporation (GSEZDC) officially handed over a designated site to Kun Huang International Industry Park Developer PLC for the development of a private industry park. This strategic collaboration designates Kun Huang as a sub-developer under the GSEZ framework, tasked with transforming a 20-hectare area into a functional industrial park within a year. The ceremonial handover was marked by the presence of GSEZ’s General Director, Mr. Motuma Temesgen, and the General Manager of Kun Huang, Mr. Sun Miao. The event was further graced by the Lume Woreda Administrator, Mr. Taye Dori, alongside other members of GSEZ’s management team, underscoring the regional significance and collaborative spirit of the initiative.
